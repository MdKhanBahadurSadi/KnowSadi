HydroVigil

Project Idea: Md Khan Bahadur Sadi

Project Type: IoT-based Water Level Monitoring System

Project Purpose: Microprocessor and Microcontroller Laboratory (CSE-3102) Course

Technology Used: ESP8266, HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor

Limitations:

  • Sensor Accuracy: The HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor may not provide accurate readings in environments with turbulence or foam on the water surface.
  • Limited Range: The ultrasonic sensor has a limited measuring range (typically 2 cm to 400 cm), which may not be suitable for monitoring very deep or large water reservoirs.
  • Wi-Fi Dependency: The ESP8266 module relies on a stable Wi-Fi connection to send notifications. In areas with poor connectivity, data transmission may fail.
  • Environmental Sensitivity: Extreme weather conditions, such as high humidity or temperature fluctuations, can affect sensor performance.
  • Maintenance Requirements: Regular maintenance is needed to clean the sensor and ensure proper operation, especially in outdoor or harsh environments.
  • Data Security: Without encryption, transmitted data may be vulnerable to interception or tampering.
  • Scalability Challenges: Adding more sensors or integrating with other systems may require significant adjustments to hardware and software.
  • Limited Battery Life: If battery-powered, the system may require frequent recharging or battery replacement, reducing its practicality for long-term use.
  • Interference Issues: Nearby objects or obstacles may cause signal reflections, leading to inaccurate distance measurements.

GhorBondhu – A Local Service Booking App for Your Neighborhood

"GhorBondhu" is a user-friendly mobile application created to connect people within a community by making local service booking easier, faster, and more organized. Designed and developed by Muhammad Khan Bahadur Sadi, this app helps users both post and request household or personal services — like electricians, tutors, cleaners, or any skilled help — directly from their smartphones.

Secure Login System: Users can register or log in using their email and password. Email verification is implemented for extra security, ensuring a trusted user base.

Dynamic Newsfeed: After logging in, users are greeted with a real-time newsfeed where they can browse available services posted by others. Each service post includes details like service title, description, price, and the poster’s contact info.

Service Posting Form: Any user can fill out a simple form to post a service they’re offering — turning the app into a platform for earning and networking within the local area.

Order Now Feature: Interested users can click on the “Order Now” button to request a service instantly, making the process smooth and interactive.

Built using: Flutter and connected to Firebase, the MVP version of GhorBondhu already includes a splash screen, authentication system, verified sign-ups, and live data loading from Firebase. The app reflects your vision of making local service-sharing simple and trusted — just like a “home-friend” (Ghor Bondhu) would be.

GhorBondhu Website – The Digital Platform for Local Services

The GhorBondhu Website is a modern, user-friendly web platform designed to make local service exchange simple, fast, and reliable. It is the web version of the GhorBondhu mobile app, aimed at helping people find or offer everyday services within their community — all from the comfort of their home.

Email Login with Verification: Users can sign up or log in using their email and password. A verification system is in place to ensure user authenticity and security.

Live Newsfeed of Services: The homepage features a real-time newsfeed where users can browse service posts submitted by others. Each post includes details like service name, description, price, and an option to order.

Service Posting Form: Any user can easily fill out a form to post a service they are offering — making it ideal for freelancers, students, or local workers to showcase their skills.

“Order Now” Button: Each service post includes an "Order Now" button that allows interested users to directly place a request for the service.

Responsive Web Design: Built using HTML, CSS, and PHP, the website is fully responsive — meaning it works smoothly on both desktop and mobile devices.
